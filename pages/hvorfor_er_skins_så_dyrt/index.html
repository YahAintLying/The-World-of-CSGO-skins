Hvorfor er skins så dyrt?

CSGO har et veldig stor samfunn for skinsene i spillet

Hva definerer prisen?

Ever since skins were introduced in the game, they began to get a lot of attention due to the loyal and passionate fanbase. With more players joining in the game and buying these skins, Valve knew it was the best thing that could happen to them and so they have been regularly introducing new skins into the game. These skins come with a case but most of the skins are usually very cheap with a few exceptions, of course. So, the chance of getting an expensive skin is pretty low and that is what defines the price of the skin, it’s a rarity! Now we already know that opening a case requires you to buy a key for a couple of bucks, but according to an analyst the chance of getting a rare item from the case is as low as 1-2%, which is extremely low and for someone like me who doesn’t even trust his own luck, I wouldn’t wanna take my chances even if it’s 50-50. Now, for a probability as low as 1%, one would have to open more than 100 crates or so to get a rare item, but then again, if you are LUCKY! So, if you decide to open up a ton of crates to get a rare item, it’d probably set you back 250 bucks or so, easily. Until you are smart enough to gather up a few bucks and buy a rare skin from a trusted marketplace like Eldorado, which would be way cheaper. And the same goes for knives in the game, the fact that they are extremely rare to be dropped in a crate is what makes them so expensive! It all comes down to an item’s rarity, the rarer the item is the more expensive it is going to be.
